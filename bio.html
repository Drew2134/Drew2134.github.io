Professional History


I am a GIS professional with 4 years experience as a GIS Specialist and Developer. I have experience in the telecommunications industry managing fiber network assets and designing maps and applications for stakeholders. I specialize in web development and field applications working to increase efficiency and automate workflows wherever possible. Most recently I've begun work in association with the FAA and have taken considerable strides as a GIS professional.

I graduated in May of 2018 from the University of Wisconsin - Oshkosh with a Bachelors in Geography and an Undergraduate Certificate in GIS. I spent time as a teaching assistant to the GIS department at the University before graduating. I am currently working towards a Masters degree in GIS Development from the University of Wisconsin to strengthen my knowledge in programming.
